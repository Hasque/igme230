Cofolios

The best way to discover design intern portfolios.

Visit Site

Visit Site
Overview

Cofolios is a website that displays design interns at top tech companies. It acts as a resource for potential applicants.

Roles

UX Design

Developer

Launch Date

October 17, 2017

Results

60K pageviews within the first 5 months. Featured on Product Hunt

The Challenge

Most companies hiring design interns tell them what they like to see in design portfolios but usually don’t provide examples. Cofolios aims to solve this problem.

Am I solving a real problem?

Quotes from interviews:
"...I can look them up through Linkedin, but that takes forever."
"It'd be nice to know if I were good enough."
"I wonder what their ideal portfolio looks like."

Before I started designing, I needed to confirm others experieced this problem. To confirm this, I interviewed design students and asked them about their pain points.

All of the designers I interviewed said they encountered the same struggles. This allowed me to proceed in research.

Selecting the Right Companies

The companies I selected were:

Design Focused -- Student designers are my audience

Popular -- Popular companies attract more students.

Intern Heavy -- The companies needed to have many interns. This would provide more references for students.

Connecting with Interns

After choosing companies I decided to start reaching out to interns that worked there. I emailed around 40 design interns asking if I could feature their portfolio on my website.

Sketches

I intended for the design simple and lightweight to put more emphasis on the companies' brand power and intern portfolios. I also used a grid layout because it would be easier to scale with.

I experimented with different layouts in my sketchbook to decided on which were the best.
